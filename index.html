Privacy Policy

K53 Learners — Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

K53 Learners is a free study app for the South African K53 learner's licence exam. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

K53 Learners does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All study progress, quiz scores, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data ever leaves your device.

No Account Required

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use it.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. We do not track your usage, location, or any other information.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not connect to any third-party services, APIs, or servers. It works entirely offline.

Data Storage

Your study progress (quiz scores, flashcard history, mock exam results) is stored only on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. It is suitable for users of all ages studying for the K53 learner's licence exam.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
